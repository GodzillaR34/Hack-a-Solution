Welcome to Foodie!

Foodie is a website and an app that allows users to find Food Trucks near their area. It's goal is to support the Food Truck Business.

Map

Find food trucks near you with Google Maps. It's easy to use.

Custom Menu!

Make your own menu by selecting the foods you purchased from food trucks!(This ability can only be performed if you have the app and have an account.)

Earn points by scanning QR Codes!

The more points you earn, the more special you are to the food truck.(This ability can only be performed if you have the app and have an account.)
